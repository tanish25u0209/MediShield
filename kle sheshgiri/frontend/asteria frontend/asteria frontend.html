Multi-image fusion
Cross-validates OCR results from up to 4 images, reducing errors and catching field mismatches between images.
Packaging validation
Detects packaging anomalies, logo mismatches, and print quality issues using the vision model.
Batch behavior analysis
Tracks scan events across time and geography. Burst detection and geo-spread catch suspicious distribution patterns.
Zero-data validation
Works without a medicine database. Date range checks, format rules, and field completeness catch obvious mismatches.
Graph intelligence
Batch scans modeled as a node graph for network-level anomaly detection beyond one image.
Drug information
Surfaces uses, side effects, and warnings from the extracted medicine name, separate from risk scoring logic.

"MediShield uses multi-image analysis and batch behavior intelligence to screen for counterfeit risk. Instead of relying only on visual AI, we analyze how a medicine behaves across scans to identify suspicious patterns early."
